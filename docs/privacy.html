Privacy Policy

Incline Financial

Who we are

Incline Financial is a financial reporting and management information service registered in England and Wales. Our registered office is in London, United Kingdom.

We can be contacted at hello@incline.financial or by telephone on 0208 137 7039.

For the purposes of UK data protection law, Incline Financial is the data controller in respect of the personal data we collect through this website.

What data we collect and why

We collect personal data only when you choose to provide it to us — for example, when you complete an enquiry form on our website. The information we collect through our contact and enquiry forms includes:

  • Your name
  • Your phone number
  • Your business name
  • Your email address
  • Your approximate annual turnover (selected from a range)
  • Your service tier interest
  • Any additional information you choose to share in the free-text field

We collect this information for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your enquiry and arrange a discovery call
  • To assess which of our services is most appropriate for your business
  • To prepare for and conduct your discovery call effectively
  • To send you a service proposal if requested

The lawful basis for processing this data is your consent — by submitting the enquiry form you are providing explicit consent for us to contact you in connection with your enquiry.

How we use your data

We use the information you provide solely to respond to your enquiry and, where relevant, to set up and conduct a discovery call. We will not use your personal data for any purpose beyond the scope of responding to your enquiry without your explicit consent.

We do not use your data for automated decision-making or profiling.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Who we share your data with

We may share your personal data with the following categories of third parties, strictly to the extent necessary to respond to your enquiry:

  • Form processing services — we use Formspree to receive and store form submissions securely. Formspree processes data in accordance with its own privacy policy and GDPR-compliant data processing terms.
  • Email service providers — your enquiry may be received via email through our email provider. We use Microsoft 365 for business email, which is GDPR-compliant.

We do not share your data with any other third parties without your explicit consent. We do not transfer your data outside the United Kingdom or European Economic Area.

How long we keep your data

If you submit an enquiry and do not become a client, we will retain your contact details for a maximum of 12 months from the date of your enquiry, after which they will be securely deleted.

If you become a client of Incline Financial, your data will be retained for the duration of our engagement and for a period of six years thereafter, in accordance with our legal and regulatory obligations.

Your rights

Under UK data protection law you have the following rights in relation to your personal data:

  • The right to access — you can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you at any time.
  • The right to rectification — you can ask us to correct any inaccurate or incomplete data we hold about you.
  • The right to erasure — you can ask us to delete your personal data in certain circumstances.
  • The right to restrict processing — you can ask us to limit how we use your data in certain circumstances.
  • The right to data portability — you can request a copy of your data in a portable format.
  • The right to withdraw consent — where we are processing your data on the basis of consent, you can withdraw that consent at any time by contacting us at hello@incline.financial.
  • The right to object — you can object to our processing of your personal data in certain circumstances.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hello@incline.financial. We will respond to all legitimate requests within one month.

Cookies

Our website does not currently use tracking cookies or analytics cookies. We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any other third-party tracking technology that would require a cookie consent banner.

If we introduce any cookies in the future — for example, as part of a Google Ads conversion tracking setup — we will update this policy and implement appropriate cookie consent mechanisms at that time.

Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. Our website is served over HTTPS and all form submissions are transmitted securely.

No method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, however, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. The date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last updated. We recommend checking this page periodically.

How to complain
